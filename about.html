About Us

Rati Beauty is an extension of our online community- makeupandbeauty.com (a.k.a. IMBB). IMBB has today grown into an international makeup and beauty community. Rati Beauty is an attempt to bring makeup to the vast community of women and impart knowledge which is not accessible to them normally.

Rati Tehri Singh, the founder of makeupandbeauty.com, is an International makeup artist, having trained under big makeup artists in India, London and Paris. Not only an expert makeup artist, she is a seasoned beauty blogger and possesses deep knowledge about products, using makeup products around the globe. With Rati Beauty, she intends to spread knowledge about makeup and makeup products amongst everyone.
